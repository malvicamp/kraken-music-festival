Directions

Bus:

The Kraken festival being located outside of Reykjavik, we have planned shuttles to take you to and from the festival. For more information on the different pick up points and routes, as well as the hours of service, please consult our pdf:

Car:

If you decide to come by car, please consult our map with step-by-step directions if you are coming from the airport or downtown Reyjavik.

Parking at the festival is $10 per day and you need to purchase tickets in advance. You will be guided to park when you reach the entrance of the park.


Accommodations

Camping:

If you are planning on camping at the festival, you need to purchase a ticket here and have the necessary equipment, such as a tent, blankets, etc. For a list of items you should bring for camping, follow this link. A ticket is good for two nights (Saturday and Sunday).
